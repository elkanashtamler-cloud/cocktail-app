    Mixology Academy

    A beginner's guide to glasses and techniques.

    Glass Types

    Highball

    Tall, narrow glass. Perfect for Gin & Tonic, Moscow Mule.

    Martini (Coupe)

    Stemmed, wide shallow bowl. Used for Martinis, Manhattans.

    Old Fashioned (Rocks)

    Short, heavy-bottomed glass. For Old Fashioned, Negroni.

    Hurricane

    Tall, curved glass. Classic for tropical drinks.

    Shot Glass

    Small. For straight spirits or layered shooters.

    Techniques

    Shaking vs. Stirring

    Shake when the drink has juice, cream, egg, or syrup. Stir for spirit-only drinks.

    ▶ Watch tutorial

    Muddling

    Crushing herbs or fruit to release flavor. Common in Mojitos and Old Fashioneds.

    ▶ Watch tutorial

    Building

    Adding ingredients directly into the serving glass. Typical for Highballs.

    ▶ Watch tutorial

    Layering

    Pouring ingredients in order so they sit in layers. Use the back of a spoon.
